## Scoreline Picks

### Panama vs England: 0:2 - Confidence: High
Basis: ESPN's full-tournament score grid also lands on 0:2. Opta had England topping Group L in 67.5% of simulations, and the market is strongly aligned with England around -330 to -370. Elo backs the mismatch too: England sit near the top tier, while Panama are a mid-table CONCACAF side by Elo. This is the cleanest favorite pick on the slate.

Risk: England could rotate if qualification is already secure, and Panama's best route is a low block that keeps the first hour ugly. Do not stretch this to 0:3 unless England still need goal difference.

### Croatia vs Ghana: 2:0 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: ESPN's score grid has Croatia 2:0, and the market makes Croatia a clear favorite while leaning under 2.5 goals. Opta's pre-tournament group view had Croatia reaching the knockouts in 76.9% of simulations, while Ghana were in the underdog tier. Elo is a major sanity check here: Croatia are far ahead of Ghana.

Risk: Ghana's opening win over Panama showed they can turn a tight game late, and any confirmed goalkeeper/injury news should be checked. If Ghana only need a draw after matchday two, 1:0 Croatia or 1:1 becomes more live.

### Colombia vs Portugal: 1:1 - Confidence: Low
Basis: ESPN's pre-tournament grid was bold on Colombia, picking Colombia 2:0, while Opta made Portugal the Group K favorite but also gave Colombia a strong advancement profile. Elo rates Colombia and Portugal very close, and current market pricing gives Portugal only a slight edge with under 2.5 shaded. The safest scoreline-pool compromise is a tight draw.

Risk: This is the trap match of the day. Portugal's name value pushes the market, but Colombia's current form and Elo profile are strong enough to punish a lazy favorite pick. Revisit after Portugal vs Uzbekistan and Colombia vs DR Congo.

### DR Congo vs Uzbekistan: 1:1 - Confidence: Low
Basis: ESPN's score grid leaned Uzbekistan 2:1, but the live market has DR Congo slightly favored and under 2.5 goals priced as the preferred total. Elo narrowly favors Uzbekistan, while DR Congo's draw with Portugal improved the case for their defensive floor. The signals split three ways, so the draw is the conservative pool play.

Risk: This match is highly table-dependent. If one team needs a win to chase a top-two or best-third slot, the late-game state could open up quickly. Wait for matchday-two results before locking it.

### Algeria vs Austria: 1:1 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: ESPN's Group J score grid picks 1:1, and Oddschecker has Austria only a slight moneyline favorite with under 2.5 goals favored. Elo rates Austria clearly stronger, but not enough to ignore Algeria's draw profile and the likelihood that this becomes a second-place control game.

Risk: Austria's press can create a 2:1 type of win if Algeria chase. If Austria enter with advancement nearly secure, the draw becomes stronger.

### Jordan vs Argentina: 0:3 - Confidence: High
Basis: ESPN's score grid picks Jordan 0:3 Argentina. Oddschecker has Argentina around -500 with over 2.5 goals favored, and Elo makes this one of the largest strength gaps of the day: Argentina are elite-tier, Jordan are a debutant-level underdog. Even with rotation, Argentina's attacking depth supports a multi-goal win.

Risk: The main risk is not the winner, it is the margin. If Argentina have already secured the group, 0:2 is safer than 0:3.

## Best Scoreline Leans

Panama vs England 0:2 is the best blend of expert scoreline, model, market, and team-strength agreement.

Jordan vs Argentina 0:3 is the best high-ceiling favorite pick, but only if Argentina still care about goal difference or group position.

## Traps / Upsets To Avoid

Do not blindly take Portugal over Colombia. The market slightly prefers Portugal, but ESPN's scoreline, Elo, and Colombia's profile all argue this is closer than the shirt reputations suggest.

Do not overrate DR Congo's market edge against Uzbekistan. DR Congo have a physical, organized profile, but Uzbekistan's Elo and ESPN's scoreline anchor both push back.

Avoid chasing a big England score before lineups. Panama can make the game slow if England rotate or if England only need a controlled result.

## Wait For Lineups

Colombia vs Portugal: wait for Portugal's front line and midfield balance. If Ronaldo starts again and Portugal look static, Colombia 1:0 or 2:1 becomes more attractive.

DR Congo vs Uzbekistan: wait for matchday-two table needs. The 1:1 pick depends on both sides respecting the downside.

Jordan vs Argentina: wait for Argentina rotation. First-choice attack points to 0:3; a rotated XI points closer to 0:2.
