### Uruguay vs Spain: 1:2 - Confidence: Medium

### Egypt vs Iran: 2:1 - Confidence: Low
Basis: Opta makes Egypt a narrow favorite at 42.9%, ahead of the draw at 32.2% and Iran at 24.9%. Sports Mole and Squawka both anchor Egypt 2-1. ESPN/DraftKings is tighter than the model, with Egypt +150 and draw +165, so this is more of a lean than a strong call.
Risk: Egypt only need a draw to secure progression, while Iran may need to chase depending on Belgium's match. Elo actually shades Iran, 31st at 1766 vs Egypt 35th at 1740, and Iran's travel constraints have eased slightly for this fixture.

### New Zealand vs Belgium: 1:3 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: Opta's strongest favorite is Belgium, winning 80.3% of simulations. ESPN/DraftKings is even more aggressive, listing Belgium -650 and -2.5 on the spread. Elo backs the mismatch: Belgium are 16th at 1869, New Zealand 74th at 1549. I am moving above Sports Mole's cautious 1-2 anchor because the model and market both point to a wider Belgium win.
Risk: Belgium's tournament attack has not fully clicked, and New Zealand have scored first in both group matches. If Belgium rotate heavily or Doku is not ready for major minutes, 1:2 is the safer fallback.
