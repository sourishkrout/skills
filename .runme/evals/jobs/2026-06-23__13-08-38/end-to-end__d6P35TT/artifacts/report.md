Risk: Canada are missing Ismael Kone after the Qatar match injury, and the expert-scoreline market is split between Canada value, Swiss control, and a mutual-benefit draw.

### Bosnia-Herzegovina vs Qatar: 2:0 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: Opta has Bosnia-Herzegovina winning 65.9% of simulations, and market odds price them as a heavy favorite. The 2:0 correct-score anchor is backed by Bosnia's attacking edge, Qatar's 6:0 loss to Canada, and Qatar suspensions to Homam El Amin and Assim Madibo.
Risk: Bosnia are winless in seven internationals and will miss Tarik Muharemovic through suspension; if Dzeko is limited, this can become a messier 2:1.

### Scotland vs Brazil: 0:2 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: Opta gives Brazil a 68.1% win probability, with Scotland at 12.9% and draw at 19.0%. Squawka's picks lean Brazil win, BTTS No, and under 2.5, while correct-score markets have Brazil 2:0 and 1:0 as the shortest prices. Elo strongly backs Brazil, 1986 to 1768.
Risk: Raphinha is ruled out, storms are a delay risk in Miami, and Scotland's best path may be damage limitation rather than opening up.

### Morocco vs Haiti: 3:0 - Confidence: High
Basis: Opta puts Morocco at 81.0% to win, with Haiti only 6.8%; Sportsgambler's correct-score pick is 3:0. Elo is a large Morocco edge, 1866 to 1528, and Racing Post notes Morocco are unbeaten in 31 matches in 90 minutes while Haiti have lost three straight.
Risk: Morocco only need to avoid trouble to progress, so rotation or game-state management could cap the margin at 2:0.

### Czechia vs Mexico: 0:1 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: Opta gives Mexico the edge at 47.9%, with Czechia 28.7% and draw 23.4%; DraftKings had Mexico favored and the under 2.5 shaded. SheKicks' correct-score anchor is Mexico 1:0, matching Mexico's two clean-sheet wins and Elo edge over Czechia, 1896 to 1696.
Risk: Mexico have top spot secured and may rotate, while Czechia must chase points; if Mexico's lineup is heavily rotated, 1:1 becomes the safer pool pick.

### South Africa vs Korea Republic: 0:2 - Confidence: Medium
Basis: No Opta match-probability article surfaced for this fixture, so this uses odds as the baseline: Korea Republic are clear favorites around 4/6, with South Africa around 5/1. Sportsgambler's correct-score pick is 0:2, and Elo backs Korea Republic 1771 to South Africa 1527.
Risk: Korea Republic may only need a draw for second place, while South Africa must win; that can create either a cautious 1:0/1:1 game or late transition chances.

## Traps / Upsets To Avoid

Do not overbuy the Canada home narrative: Canada can top the group with a draw, and Switzerland remain the model/market favorite.

Do not force a big Mexico scoreline: Mexico's defense is reliable, but rotation risk is real with Group A already won.

Do not chase Scotland upset sentiment: Scotland's advancement story is real, but the Brazil model gap is still large.

## Wait For Lineups
